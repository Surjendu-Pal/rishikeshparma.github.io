About Me

About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Operations Management at the University of Luxembourg, and I expect to defend my dissertation in September 2025. My doctoral research, conducted under the supervision of Prof. Çağıl Koçyiğit and Prof. Benny Mantin, focuses on optimization under uncertainty. Specifically, I address resource allocation challenges in supply chain problems by designing simple, practical algorithms. My work combines optimization methods with machine learning techniques to develop solutions that are both efficient and implementable in real-world settings.

I hold an Integrated Master's degree in Mathematics and Computing from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Dhanbad, awarded in 2019. During my studies, I was a visiting research intern at the University of Salford, where I worked under the guidance of Prof. Phil Scarf.

About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Operations Management at the University of Luxembourg, expecting to defend my dissertation in September 2025. My doctoral research, supervised by Prof. Çağıl Koçyiğit and Prof. Benny Mantin, focuses on optimization under uncertainty. Specifically, I address resource allocation challenges in supply chain problems by designing simple and practical algorithms. My work integrates optimization techniques with machine learning methods to develop solutions that are not only efficient but also suitable for real-world implementation.

I hold an Integrated Master’s degree in Mathematics and Computing from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Dhanbad, awarded in 2019. During my studies, I was a visiting research intern at the University of Salford, where I worked under the mentorship of Prof. Phil Scarf.
